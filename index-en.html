Science Insights

Controlled breathing exercises are simple and effective tools to directly influence the nervous system, promote relaxation, and improve overall health.

Benefits of each exercise:

  • Relax: The most effective way to slow down heart rate and stimulate the vagus nerve.
  • Sigh: Quickly resets the system during sudden panic, palpitations, or acute stress.
  • Balance: Stabilizes and balances emotions over the long term for daily maintenance.
  • 4-7-8: Acts as a natural sedative for the nervous system to ease into quick sleep.
  • Box: Increases mental clarity, situational awareness, and deep concentration.
  • Box Pro: Intense version to strengthen self-control and lung capacity.
